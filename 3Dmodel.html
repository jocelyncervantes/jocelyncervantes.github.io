Angel Crab

"spirit animal", Sep 29, 2020

This art was done as a Art-74 assignment at SJSU

In this art piece I created a custom 3D model using adobe dimensions and meshmixer with 3D sets I found online for free. After downloading the free to use crab and angel wings, I used mesh mixer to combine both things and blend them together. After I had the general model I used adobe dimensions to add color, texture, and a background to my angel crab. The story behind the angel crab was your quote on quote,spirit animal communing to you in the form of a saviour. A goal I had was to make the crab appear to be angelic and soft. Overall I think that the final product came out looking adorable considering how scary crabs can be.
